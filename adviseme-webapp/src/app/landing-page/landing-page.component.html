About

The idea for AdviseMe started during the summer of 2017. A group of rising seniors at the University of South Carolina were discussing ideas for their senior Capstone project. A major problem that everyone had faced during their college careers was communication issues with their advisors. An idea was then proposed that if there was a simple web app that could standardize the otherwise haphazard advisement process then students could feel more confident in their course choices and avoid a lot of stress. This simple idea was turned into AdviseMe.

Starting in the Fall 2017 semester the team, captained by Tyler Hall, hit the ground running and now near the end of the Spring 2018 semester has a working advisement communication tool!
